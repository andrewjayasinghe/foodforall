ABOUT US

At Food for All, we believe in returning to nature's bounty for human nourishment In the tropical paradise of Sri Lanka, we are blessed with an abundance of seasonal fruits & vegies. At Food for All, we transform this fresh produce into wholesome, natural snacks that can be enjoyed by everyone. Our dehydrated fruity and vegie snacks range offer a healthier alternative, promoting new snacking- habits that shift us away from artificial treats towards a nutrient-rich, health-promoting options. Join us on a journey to nourish with the goodness that nature has provided, rejuvenating our bodies and souls with natural enzymes, vitamins and antioxidants.
